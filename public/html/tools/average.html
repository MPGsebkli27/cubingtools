WCA Average Calculator

This tool provides an interactive tool for cubing enthusiasts to track their solve times, calculate averages, and monitor their personal ranks (PR) for different cubing events. You input your solve times, which are then processed and displayed in real-time on the site. The tool fetches personal rank data from the World Cube Association, showing how each solve compares to your historical results. After five solves, the site calculates the average and saves it as a "tag" along with the individual times and creation date. This allows you to retain a record of past averages and view them in a structured list, which can be managed and updated as they continue to improve.
